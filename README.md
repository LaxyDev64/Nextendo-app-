## 👾 What is Nextendo Network?

Nextendo Network is a **from-scratch reimplementation** of the online services behind several Nintendo Switch games, built so people can keep playing together. It runs its own account system, matchmaking servers and friends/presence layer — and plugs into a customized Ryujinx build **and** real CFW consoles.

- 🎮 **Real online play** — matchmaking, private rooms, friends, presence.
- 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 **One identity everywhere** — a single Nextendo account for the emulator *and* your Switch, with a permanent friend code.
- 🔒 **Privacy-first** — no Nintendo account, no ads, no tracking. Your saves stay yours.
- 🆓 **Free, forever.**

## 🚀 Get started

1. **[Download Ryujinx-Nextendo](https://github.com/NextendoNetwork/Ryujinx-Nextendo/releases/latest)** and unzip it.
2. Launch it — the quick-start wizard sets up your games folder, keys and online profile.
3. Create a free **[Nextendo account](https://nextendo.network)** — required for online — link it, then launch a supported game and find the online modes.

> Playing on a real Switch? Grab the **[Nextendo `.nro`](https://github.com/NextendoNetwork/nextendo-nro/releases/latest)** instead.
